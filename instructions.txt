Chocolate cake instructions

preheat oven to 450 deg Farenheit.
combine all ingredients.
best to start with dry incredients. mix we and dry ingredients separately.
add wet ingredients TO bowl with dry ones. DO NOT do this the other way round as it will result in lumps.
Use mixer to mix the ingredients.
once all mixed, add 1 cup boiling water adding a bit of water at a time.
add to lines baking bans
bake for 35 mins
take out and poke with fork/toothpick to ensure middle of cake is baked through.
if fully backed, return to oven rack for another 2 mins-5 mins
